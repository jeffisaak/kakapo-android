Why am I seeing this message?

When you create a new account, your device generates a pair of keys to use for encryption, decryption, and authentication. It then attempts to save the keypair so that it can be retrieved and used in the future. For some reason, your device failed to properly save the new keypair.

What should I do?

It is unlikely that trying again is going to make a difference. If you'd like to email us and tell us what happened, that might help us figure out what went wrong.

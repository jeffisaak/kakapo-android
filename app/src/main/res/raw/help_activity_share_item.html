Overview

To submit a new item, select one or more friends and/or groups to share the item with, enter a title, and one or more of a URL, attachment, and message, then tap the send button at the top of the screen.

Selecting friends and groups

To select friends and/or groups to share with, start typing the friend/group name in the 'Share with' text field. Once you type two characters, the friends and groups that match will be shown in a list and you can select one. To add another friend or group, start typing the next friend or group name.

Queued items

If you submitted a new item and the upload failed for some reason, the reason will be shown at the top of the screen on a red background. If this is the case, you may also delete the item by tapping the delete button at the top of the screen.

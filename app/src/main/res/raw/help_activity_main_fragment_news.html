Overview

This screen contains your news feed; all of the things that you share and all of the things that are shared with you will be listed here. From this screen you can filter your news feed, read items and create and upload new items to share with your friends.

Refreshing the list

When new things are shared with you they are not automatically downloaded; in order to see the latest items, scroll to the top of the list and then swipe down to refresh the list.

Filtering the list

You can filter the list to include your items, your friends items, and strangers items (a stranger is someone that has you as a friend but you don't have them as a friend). You can also show or hide deleted items and ignored items.

Item details

To view an item and to read any responses that other friends may have shared to it, tap on it in the list. If the item has an attached photo, you may tap on the thumbnail to view a full-size image.

Long-press

You can long-press on an item to perform operations on the item. If you long-press, a menu will appear that allows you to delete an item, add the author of an item as a friend, and ignore both the author and the individual item.

Turning a stranger into a friend

It is possible for someone to have you as a friend when you don't have them as a friend. In Kakapo, these people are called strangers. You can turn a stranger into a friend by finding something that they have shared and adding them as a friend. To do this from the news feed, long-press on an item and select 'Add friend' from the popup menu that appears. Give the friend a name, and you're set.

Ignoring people and items

You can ignore both individual items and all items by specific people. If you have ignored someone, nothing they share will be shown in your newsfeed unless you choose to view ignored items using the filter. If you change your mind about ignoring someone or something, you can long-press on the item after showing it using the filter and unignore either the author or item.

Creating new items

To share a new item with your friends, tap on the add button at the bottom of the screen and a new screen will open allowing you to set a title and message, share a web site URL, and upload images.

Failed shares

If you create a new item and the upload fails, it will be displayed in the news feed with a note indicating failure. You may tap on it and see the detailed reason it failed and have options to modify the item and resend it.

Signing out

To sign out of your account and return to the sign in/sign up screen, tap the sign out button.

Why am I seeing this message?

You are trying to add a friend to Kakapo, but we couldn't find a user with the ID you specified. Perhaps the friend you are trying to add removed their Kakapo account, or you may be trying to add a friend from the clipboard, but what is on your clipboard isn't a Kakapo ID.

What should I do?

If you're adding a friend from the clipboard, ensure that what is on the clipboard is a friend ID. If that is the case, you can check with the person you're trying to add to see if they still have a Kakapo account.

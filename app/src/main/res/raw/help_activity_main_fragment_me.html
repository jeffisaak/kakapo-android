Overview

This screen contains some basic information about your account, such as your ID, colour, quota usage, and any queued items that haven't been uploaded to the Kakapo server. It allows you to perform operations on your account, such as share your ID with friends, and backup or delete your account.

My ID

This is how you are identified to the Kakapo server and to people that you share things with. It is unique to you.

You may share your ID with your friends by showing a QR code on the screen (which your friends may then scan using their device) or by sending it to them via email or a messaging service like SMS, WhatsApp, or Signal (we like Signal). Tap the share button at the top of your screen, then either of the sharing options in the submenu that is displayed to share your ID.

Colour

This is a colour that is used on your device to highlight things you share using Kakapo. It is not shared with your friends, as they may choose a different colour on their device for you. Tap on the colour to select a new one.

Queued items

If you have created new items that did not get successfully uploaded to the Kakapo server, they wil be listed on this screen. You can edit them by tapping on them, or delete or resubmit them by long-pressing on them and selecting the appropriate option from the menu that appears.

My quota

When you share stuff with Kakapo, it is stored on the Kakapo server in encrypted form. Everything you share takes up a certain amount of disk space on our servers, and you are allocated a maximum. This will show you how much disk space you are using on the server and what your maximum is.

If you reach your quota, you will not be able to share anything unless you first delete something. Deleting stuff you've shared removes it from the Kakapo server and reduces the amount of disk space you use.

Deleting your account

You may delete your account just from your device or from your device and from our servers. Deleting your account from your device may be reversed by restoring your account from a backup (if you have one). Deleting your account from our servers is irreversible. When you delete your account from our servers all of the things you have shared will be deleted as well.

To delete your account, tap the delete button (the trashcan) at the top of your screen, then select whether you'd like to delete the account from just your device or also our servers from the menu that appears. You will be asked to enter your password and must do so successfully before your account is deleted.

Renaming your account

Your account name is only used on your device, and can be anything you like. It is not shared with anyone; your friends will assign a name to you on their device. Tap the rename account button at the top of the screen and enter a new name to rename your account.

Sharing your account to another device

The easiest way to copy your account to another device is to share it. Please see the share accounts help for details.

Backing up your account

Backing up your account is important and is another way to share your account to another device. You'll find detailed instructions on the backup help page.

Signing out

To sign out of your account and return to the sign in/sign up screen, tap the sign out button.

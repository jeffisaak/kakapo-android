Quick help

This screen shows a list of the Kakapo accounts you have on your device. From here you may sign in, delete accounts, and add new accounts.

More details

There are two parts to a Kakapo account. One part is stored on your device, and the other part is stored on our servers. This screen lists the accounts that are stored on your device.

You may read more about Kakapo accounts and how they work.

Signing in

You may sign in with one of the listed accounts. To do this, tap on the account in the list and enter the password for the account when prompted.

Creating a new account

To import or create a Kakapo account, tap the add button near the bottom of the screen; you will be shown a list of three options for adding accounts. To add a new account, tap the 'Add new account' button. Enter a name and password for the account and tap 'OK' and the new account will be created and added to the list of accounts.

Adding an account from another device

Read more about sharing your account with another device.

Restoring an account from a backup

Read more about restoring accounts.

Deleting accounts

You may delete accounts by tapping on the delete button beside the name of the account. You will have to enter the password for the account before it may be deleted. You may either delete the account only from the device you are using, or you may delete the account from our servers as well.

If you delete your account from your device without a backup, you will not be able to recover the account. If you delete the account from our servers, the account is permanently removed and may not be restored, even if you have a backup.

When you delete your account from our servers, all of the things that you have shared will be deleted as well, and any responses you make to other people will be removed.

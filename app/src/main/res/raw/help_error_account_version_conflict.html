Why am I seeing this message?

The encrypted account information on the server is newer than the account data you are attempting to upload to the server.

How does this happen?

If you have your Kakapo account on two devices and make changes on both of them (for exmaple by adding or renaming a friend), one of them will update the server first. The second one to do so will be unable.

What should I do?

Exit and restart Kakapo. Account data is synchronized in a few places, most notably on app startup, and updated account information will be downloaded and updated on your device when you restart the app.

Quick Help

To restore your backed up account, locate the backup file that you saved when you backed up your account and open it on your device. When asked what app to open it with, select 'Restore Kakapo Account'.

More details

To restore an account, you must first have made a backup of your account. When you create a backup, Kakapo allows you to share it using the sharing capabilities of your device. Read more about backing up your account.

Wherever you saved your account backup, go find it. If you emailed it to yourself, open your email app and locate the email. If you saved it on your device, find the file with a file explorer app (we like Amaze). Or if you saved it to the cloud, locate the file with your cloud app, such as Google Drive or Nextcloud.

Once you've located the file, open or share it. Your device will show you a list of ways that you can open/share the file. One of them should be 'Restore Kakapo Account'. Select that, and Kakapo will open the backup file and ask you for the password that you used to encrypt the backup. This is not the same password as your account password, although there is nothing stopping you from using the same password for your account and for encrypting your backups.

Once you've entered the correct password, you'll be shown a summary of the account. If you decide to proceed with the restore, tap the checkbox at the top of the screen and the account will be restored to your device. You can then log in to the account and use it normally.

Share account

There are two ways to share your account to another device: By scanning a QR code or by copying and pasting a long text string. Scanning the QR code is easier and is recommended for everyone, but if the device you are adding the account to doesn't have a camera, you may have to copy and paste the backup string instead.

A note on encryption

When your account details are stored on the Kakapo server, they are encrypted using your account password. We are unable to decrypt your account details, even if we wanted to.

Sharing with QR codes

To share your account to another device using QR codes, first sign in to your Kakapo account on the first device, navigate to the 'Me' screen, select the 'Share' action at the top of the screen, and select 'Share my account as QR Code'. A QR code will be shown on the screen.

Then, using the second device, ensure you are at the 'Sign in/sign up' screen, tap the add button near the bottom of the screen, then select 'Add account from QR code' and scan the QR code currently shown on the first device. You will be prompted to enter the account password. Upon successfully entering the password, the account will be added to the list of accounts and you may use it to sign in.

Sharing with text

The process is very similar to sharing with QR codes, except when sharing the account, select 'Share my account as text'. You will then have to get the text to whatever device you want to add the account to, whether by instant messaging, email, or what you.

On the other device, at the 'Sign in/sign up' screen, select 'Add account from clipboard' and then enter your account password.

Friends

This screen lists your friends. If you tap on one of your friends you can see some details regarding the friend and modify the groups that they are members of. You can also add friends from this screen.

Adding friends

To add a friend to Kakapo, tap the add button, then tap either the 'Add friend from QR code' button (if you want to scan a friend's ID with your camera) the 'Add friend from clipboard' button (if you've copied your friends ID to the clipboard), or the 'Add friend manually' button (if you want to type your friends ID in manually).

You will then be prompted to name your friend. This can be whatever you choose, and needn't be your friends real name. It is only present on your device.

Friend details

If you tap on one of your friends, you will be shown some details about the friend, including their ID, their colour, and which groups they are members of. From the friend details you can change their colour, modify the groups that they are members of, share the friend's ID, or rename or delete the friend.
